# Legal Services & Consultation Website

A comprehensive resource for legal, compliance, and business registration services in Delhi and New Delhi.

## Overview

This website provides information and guidance on various legal and business services including:

- **Criminal Law**: Expert consultation on criminal cases and legal representation
- **Business Compliance**: Company compliance and legal advisory services
- **Business Registration**: UDYAM, MSME, and trade license registration assistance
- **Tax & Compliance**: ITR and GST filing services

- **Specialized Services**: Cheque bounce cases, banking laws, and more
