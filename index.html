About the Advocate

Dedicated to Justice & Your Rights

Advocate Jiji is an enrolled member of the Bar Council of Delhi, practising before the Hon'ble Supreme Court of India, the High Court of Delhi, various District Courts, and Tribunals.

Educated at a premier National Law University, Advocate Jiji brings a sharp analytical mind, strong courtroom presence, and a compassionate approach to every matter. Special emphasis is placed on understanding each client's unique circumstances and crafting strategies that protect their interests.
